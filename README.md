## ğŸ¯ Project Overview & Problem Statement

Agricultural activities often suffer from inefficiency due to a lack of data-driven planning. Farmers struggle with:
* **Supply/Demand Mismatch:** Difficulty in choosing the right crops based on market needs.
* **Uncontrollable Factors:** Sudden weather changes (floods, droughts) without early warning.
* **Communication Gaps:** Lack of direct channels with the Agriculture Directorate.
* **Resource Management:** Inefficient use of water and soil due to lack of education.

**Ã§iftÃ§iMobil** addresses these issues by creating a digital platform that provides real-time data, educational resources, and direct communication channels.
